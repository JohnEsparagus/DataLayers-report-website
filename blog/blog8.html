Blog Update

19/02/2025

  • 5 days ago
  • 1,024

We have a group of school kids coming into the labs to test our product. Two groups tried our projects, one doing A-Level computer science and one doing BTEC IT. We had them mess around with our project and explore our widget.

We then asked about design features we could implement, and surprisingly they had a lot to say. Many ideas we never thought of initially but will now implement, for example keyboard shortcuts and accessibility features. They were ruthless with their critique but overall enjoyed using our product.

[insert pic]

Levon and Lenart were showing off the widget, and Amir was taking notes of their answers to our questions to graph and implement improvements later on.

This was a good experience as it was the first time others came to try our widget with us present. It is nearing the end of our implementation part of our project, we are focused on getting our product ready for our clients, and then working on our report extensively.
