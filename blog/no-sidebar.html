Blog Update

October 1

In October 2024, our team Lenart, Eric, Levon, and Amir came together to set up our widget project. Our initial focus was on defining our goals and aligning our vision, which required extensive discussions and collaboration. A key part of this phase was actively communicating with clients to understand their needs and expectations.

To ensure we built a user-centric product, we conducted an in depth study on user requirements as a part of our systems engineering Research. This involved analyzing key aspects of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), including watching Lecture Videos, followed by hands-on Lab.

We also dedicated time to practical exercises, refining our skills in sketching user experiences, prototyping, and applying core design principles. These activities helped us translate theoretical knowledge into actionable design strategies, setting a solid foundation for the development phase.

As we move forward, our focus will remain on user experience, iteration, and refining our widget based on feedback and best practices. Stay tuned for more updates!

Clients

Additionally, we have been thinking about our users, and dealing with clients. There were various meetings with prof Deean Mohamedally, our mentrs for the project to ensure our goals and motivations were aligned. We also had a meeting with the clients, who were very excited about our project and gave us some feedback on what they would like to see in the final product.

It gave us a real feel for working on real projects, the importance of communication and understanding the needs of the clients.
